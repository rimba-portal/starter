# Rimba Portal

## Resource Integration for Manufacturing & Business Alignment

Rimba is an integrated manufacturing and business operations platform designed to connect people, processes, assets, knowledge, compliance, and execution within a single ecosystem.

Rather than deploying multiple disconnected systems, organizations can use Rimba as a unified operational backbone.

## Core Capabilities

* Organization Structure Management
* Workforce Planning
* Workflow Automation
* Task Management
* Document Management
* Learning & Competency Management
* Asset & Maintenance Management
* Risk & CAPA Management
* Vendor & Contract Management

## Key Benefits

* Single Source of Truth
* Reduced Software Fragmentation
* Improved Operational Visibility
* Faster Decision Making
* Better Compliance & Audit Readiness
* Scalable Modular Architecture

## Manufacturing Focus

Rimba is designed specifically for manufacturers seeking to standardize operations, improve governance, and scale efficiently.

## Open Core Platform

The Rimba Foundation is freely available and provides the core building blocks required to digitize organizational operations.

Organizations can extend the platform through premium modules, industry templates, content packs, and integrations.

## Vision

To become the operational digital backbone for manufacturing organizations worldwide.
